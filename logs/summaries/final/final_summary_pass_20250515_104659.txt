Topic: Breast infection/sepsis symptoms
Keywords: er, infection, like, breast, people, going, red, said, sepsis, weeks

CHUNK SUMMARIES:
Chunk 1:
If you notice a red spot on one breast that isn’t going away, if you have new orange-peel-like dimpling, or one breast appears swollen, can be a sign of inflammatory breast cancer.
